About Us

CapeTownMagazine is a digital publication dedicated to showcasing the best culture and lifestyle in the Mother City.
We highlight events, attractions, and stories that inspire both locals and international visitors.

Our Story

CapeTownMagazine.com began as a small online project with the aim of sharing Cape Town’s culture and lifestyle.
Over time, it grew into one of the city’s most trusted digital platforms, attracting hundreds of thousands of readers each month.
Today, it serves both locals and international visitors by offering insider tips, guides, and stories that highlight the best of Cape Town.

The Magazine

The magazine’s purpose is to create joyful experiences by connecting people with unique events, attractions, and businesses.
Instead of focusing on advertising, it emphasizes storytelling giving readers something new and exciting to discover every day.
With a growing global audience, CapeTownMagazine has become a key voice in promoting Cape Town as a vibrant destination.

Our Vision

Our vision is to remain the most reliable and engaging online guide to Cape Town.
By showcasing hidden gems, supporting local entrepreneurs, and highlighting cultural experiences,
the magazine aims to make Cape Town a city that inspires both residents and visitors.

Readership

CapeTownMagazine reaches a diverse audience, from young adults to families, and from locals to international tourists.
Its weekly newsletter keeps tens of thousands of subscribers updated with fresh content,
while the website itself draws millions of impressions each month.
